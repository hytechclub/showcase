Freestyle

Freestyle is an event in swimming where you can swim any legal stroke, but people usually swim the "front crawl", as it is the fastest stroke. Until the 2028 olympics, this is the only event in professional swimming where you can swim 50 yards, but also the only event where you can swim a mile (almost).
