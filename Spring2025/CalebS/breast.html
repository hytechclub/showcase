Breaststroke

Breaststroke is the slowest stroke in swimming. It takes a lot of finesse to swim this stroke, and even though it doesn't seem like it, it takes a lot of strength and endurance to swim this stroke long distances. Breaststroke is different than all of the other strokes in the way that it has what is called a "pull out". In a pull out, after you push off of the blocks and do streamline underwater, you are allowed to do one dolphin kick, and then you pull down with your arms.
