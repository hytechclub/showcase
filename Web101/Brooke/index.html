Adaptation Ratings

My reviews of different video game adaptations that I've seen over the years.

List of adaptations that will be on this website:

  • Angry Birds Movie
  • Kirby: Right Back at Ya!
  • Sonic the Hedgehog
  • Super Mario Bros. (1993. They may be making a new one.)
  • Pokemon (I'm going to put them all in here as one.)
  • Detective Pikachu

Note: I have seen more, though these are the only ones on the list. I only chose one adaptation per game. Some of these ratings may also be tainted by nostalgia.

Sonic the Hedgehog

Game Move or Show? Years it Ran / Release Year # of Seasons / Runtime Rating / 10 Stars
Sonic the Hedgehog Movie 2020 1h 39m 7
"Fun to watch, but missing something for Sonic enjoyers."

As I have said in my quote, the movie was fun with a good plot and had many jokes that Sonic fans would understand over the average viewer, though I feel like some of the world could have had more "Sonic" to it, similar to how the TV show Sonic X brought Sonic to the human world. It looks like the second movie will have more for the fans, though I haven't seen it yet. This movie has potential to do a lot with the proper care and the team seems to care due to their fixes of Sonic's original design when the trailers came out.

Super Mario Bros.

Game Move or Show? Years it Ran / Release Year # of Seasons / Runtime Rating / 10 Stars
Super Mario Bros. Movie 1993 1h 44m 1
"A true disgrace to Mario."

I would have used the Super Mario Brothers Super Show as the reveiw for mario because I have more nostalgia for that one, but I decided to review the Super Mario Bros. Movie from 1993 because I have seen it and want to share my pain with the world. I saw it once when I was young, meaning I had a lower standard for movies and shows, and I thought, "Wow. This is not Mario." The movie is trying to be something that Mario isn't, which is the downfall of the movie. It is not Mario and anyone who would want to watch this movie could see that from the start.
