Pantone Color of the Year

Each year, Pantone chooses a color that they predict will become incorporated into trends and lifestyles of the entire population around the globe. It indicates to stores that the color is selling and will appear everywhere from clothing to furniture. Therefore, it is very important to interior designers because it dictates what colors and designs are in style for that year and it gives them ideas as to what trends clients will want.


Paint: What's in it?

Pigment: colors the paint, hides the color behind it on the wall, and makes paint thicker


Solvent: a liquid allow the paint to move from the can to the surface; the solvent and the binder are what create the main part of paint


Binder Solids: hold the pigment together to create a dry film on the surface it is being painted on

Minimalist Interior Design: Embracing Simplicity

Minimalist interior design, born in the mid-20th century, champions simplicity and functionality. Rooms feature neutral colors like whites and grays, promoting a serene atmosphere and maximizing space and light. Furniture is chosen for clean lines and minimalism, often with integrated storage solutions. Emphasizing spaciousness, minimalist design uses ample negative space and favors natural light through large windows. This style aims to create tranquil, uncluttered environments that foster a sense of calm and clarity in daily living.
