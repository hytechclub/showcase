OnlineXP

v0.6.09

A virtual machine of the beloved OS, Windows XP, for your browser. Windows XP was released in 2001 and became almost everyone's top choice until the early to mid 2010s, when the shift to Windows 7 began. Anybody, whether they had the system or not, could recognize its iconic start-up sound and grassy hill wallpaper. It wasn't until 2014 that Microsoft ended support for Windows XP, with the last update arriving in 2019, marking its end of life. Immerse yourself in the nostalgia of the 2000s, a time when life was simpler and everything seemed wonderful. We hope you enjoy our virtual machine, and remember to archive it before Microsoft takes it down.

PS: This virtual machine is still in its beta stage, so expect bugs, application errors, and/or crashes.
